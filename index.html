About

Passionate about video games and their development, focused on art and visual effects.

I am Alicia, a university student incapable of committing to a single branch of knowledge. I chose to study Game Design and Development because it allowed me to learn about countless topics, delving into them at my own pace, while also combining my artistic and technical potential.

Art is one of the most special branches for me; I love creating illustrations, characters, objects, and visual effects, allowing me to translate an abstract concept onto paper and the screen.

Programming and the technical aspects enable me to see these creations come to life within video games, opening the door to a fourth dimension for art and providing features that cannot be captured in 2D or 3D alone.

This is why visual effects and technical art are my favorite fields in game development, as they allow me to leverage both aspects in a way that immerses the player in a world designed and created to provide a unique and unforgettable experience.

Expertise

My key areas of expertise.

I specialize in various areas of game design and development, ranging from the technical aspects such as programming and visual effects to the more creative fields like conceptual art and 3D design.

I have experience using various programs commonly employed in the video game industry.

  • Software for editing and creating 2D graphics and animations, both raster and vector.

  • Software for 3D modeling, rigging, skinning, and animation.

  • Game engines for programming, visual effects, and technical art.

UI Design

Designer and developer of user interfaces and menus, with experience in designing, creating, and programming intuitive and appealing solutions to enhance the user experience in video games.

Proficient in raster and vector image programs, as well as in implementing UI in game engines.

Expert in creating original visual styles that seamlessly integrate with the overall aesthetics and themes of each video game, as well as adapting existing visual styles to menus and HUDs.

2D Art

Artist with experience in 2D game art, specializing in conceptual art for characters and environments, with extensive knowledge of their design.

Experienced in creating textures, illustrations, and 2D animations, with expertise in exporting and importing assets in game engines.

Skilled at delivering various drawing styles, including pixel art, cartoons, and realism. Experienced in both traditional and digital art, with expertise in using various drawing and painting techniques.

3D Art

Broad skills in the 3D creation process, with experience in modeling, unwrapping, and texturing characters, objects, and environments.

3D artist proficient in rigging and skinning characters, as well as animating characters and objects using various techniques and tools in 3D software.

Experienced in programming physics for video games, ranging from cloth and spring simulations to rigid and deformable solids.

Visual Effects

Experience in designing and developing particle systems in game engines. High capacity to recreate animations and visual particle compositions to enhance gameplay experiences.

Knowledgeable in color theory and composition applied to visual effects. Expertise in creating cartoon and stylized realistic visual effects.

Extensive experience in crafting custom shaders to achieve desired visual effects for different occasions and video games.

Technical Art

Advanced knowledge of the graphics pipeline and its phases, with experience in game shader programming and mathematics applied to computer graphics.

Developer with experience in creating procedurally animated textures and materials with specific visual styles for games, ranging from semi-realistic to cartoon.

Skills in importing assets into game engines and adjusting them to meet the requirements of each video game.

Programming

Developer with experience in game programming, with expertise in gameplay programming, game algorithms, and user interfaces. Knowledgeable in network and multiplayer game programming.

Proficient in object-oriented and event-driven languages such as Java, C++, C#, and Javascript, as well as shading languages like GLSL and HLSL.

Skilled in programming physics in game engines for simulations involving cloth, springs, rigid bodies, and deformable objects.

Portfolio

I have had the privilege of working on these incredible projects.

As a university student in Game Design and Development, I've had the pleasure of creating numerous personal and collaborative projects with multidisciplinary teams, both within the academic setting and beyond.

All these projects have allowed me to develop my skills in game design and development, enabling me to initiate and later refine my competencies in various areas such as 2D and 3D art, user interface design, visual effects, or gameplay programming, among others.

Some projects have even led to the development of award-winning games with opportunities for travel and exhibitions at different national and international festivals, such as the Guerrilla Game Festival 2023 in Madrid or Gamescom 2023 in Cologne.
